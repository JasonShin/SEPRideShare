About Us

We designed ShareTheRide to solve the problem of inefficient travel in the Sydney area. We wanted to give people the opportunity to use resources that already exist. There are 4 million people in Sydney.

Lots of them are routinely driving back and forth to and from the same locations. We've created a system which enables Sydney to pool its resources together so that we can save eachother's time by Sharing The Ride.
